Courses with the red cart are available but not now because in the first semester you cannot choose them. They can e seen by following the steps above and selecting a semetser.

Under **Primary study path** you can see each semester and which courses you can take for each one.

The name `computer systems and networks` is arbitrary it basically means computer science and the courses changed over the years.

After first semesster it takes only 20 ects to pass.

Random processes is a math course since within our masters program we have some mandatory courses from some other studies.

If you pass a course that is within a specific type of course that must be passed you’re not required to pass such a course anymore in the masters for instance if you are getting a course that is a type of mathematics and pass it like random processes in the first semester you do not have to take another mathematics course in the masters.

If you do not pass a course that is every other semester than you have to wait a whole semester to pass it. You cannot swap out a course with another course at this school, this is possible when you do it in an erasmus though!

You have to find a professor that teaches a subject that is of your interest and can ask them to be your instructor if they have a research that is interesting for you.

Topics for thesis can be found on the link: [https://apd.usos.pw.edu.pl/topics/browse/](https://apd.usos.pw.edu.pl/topics/browse/) and then searching for `IN-SSK` in the field for `Study field` and searching for `Thesis type` of masters.
