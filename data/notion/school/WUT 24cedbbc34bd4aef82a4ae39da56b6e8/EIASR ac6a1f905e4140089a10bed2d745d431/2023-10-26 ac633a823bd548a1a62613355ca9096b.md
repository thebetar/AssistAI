Sound is meassured as a fourier wave, fourier transformations turn the fourier wave into separate waves that originally got combined to create the fourier wave. This is done by some calculations called the discrete fourier transformation.

With a discrete fourier transformation we can take a fourier wave and convert it back to the original frequency waves that it was made out of. The fast fourier transformation is a faster way to do this transformation.

There are different version of the fast fourier transformation which each have their own specific case when they are more efficient than the other. To understand these at a basic level the fourier waves and the discrete fourier transformation need to be understood.
