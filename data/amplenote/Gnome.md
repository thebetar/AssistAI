Gnome is a desktop environment used on [Linux](Linux.md) based systems. The goal of Gnome is to make linux based systems very usable and understandable for the average user. It has succeeded in this greatly over the years. Gnome is most well known for being the desktop environment used by [Ubuntu](Ubuntu.md).

Gnome also offers a lot of visual tools to change settings within a system.
