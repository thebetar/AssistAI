# Operations

Operations are also a very important aspect of being a developer. I have had many instances where it was not part of my daily work to do anything with operations but knowing what happens when you push your code forward is very important so you know when something goes wrong what has caused it or so you know how to deliver your code best.

In some cases I have been responsible for the operations of a project so I can speak with some expertise on this subject.
