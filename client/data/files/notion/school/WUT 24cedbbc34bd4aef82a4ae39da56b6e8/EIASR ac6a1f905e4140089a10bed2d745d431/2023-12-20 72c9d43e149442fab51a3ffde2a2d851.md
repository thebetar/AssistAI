**Bayes** Bayes theorem is about changing the probability after learning of a prior. Simply said this is for instance changing the probability having fun on a holiday, which goes up after hearing from a colleague that they had fun in the same destination. This is denoted by $P(B | A) = {P(B) * P(A | B) \above{1pt} P(A)}$

Bayes rule is the most popular way to calculate the probability with a conditional.
