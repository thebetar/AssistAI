# Math

This is a page dedicated to writing down information about mathematical problems that relate to programming. Mostly about efficiency and scaling. In the current world this is a highly valuable skill to obtain since a lot of companies currently struggle with high costs for cloud computing due to unoptimsed code.

## Math

Linear algebra is very important when calculating how many operations a for loop or something similar has to do. On a small scale lets say over an array or list of 10 items this does not really matter (in most cases). But once you scale up to millions even billions of requests a 10% increase all of a sudden really starts to matter.
